About Me

Web Development

Hey there! My name is Corina and for the past 4 years have been working as a UX/UI Designer. I’m now looking to broaden my skillset and am working to do so within Thinkful’s Software Engineering bootcamp.

I first discovered the realm of development at the age of 12,  when I created and sold graphics and coded up - wait for it-  Neopets profile pages for myself and my friends. Element styling was, horrifyingly, done inline at the time. However, the spark was lit- and I’ve been taking every opportunity to work with code ever since.

I’m most excited about working in teams to solve large-scale business problems. I'll be learning the following technologies: HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, NodeJS, Express, and React.
